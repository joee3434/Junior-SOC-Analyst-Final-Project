# REAL SIEM ALERTS – SOC101

## Introduction
This section covers the analysis of phishing alerts detected by a SIEM system as part of a SOC analyst workflow.

## Alert Overview
- Alert Name: SOC101 – Phishing Mail Detected
- Severity: Low
- Source System: Exchange
- Alert Type: Email Phishing

## Investigation Summary
Multiple phishing alerts were detected over a period of several months. Although classified as low severity, phishing alerts require investigation to prevent potential compromise.

## Initial Findings
The alerts indicate potential malicious emails attempting to deceive users into clicking malicious links or providing sensitive information.

## Next Steps
Further analysis will include email header inspection, IOC extraction, and correlation with network traffic.
